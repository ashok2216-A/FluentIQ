Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is FluentIQ compared to human tutors?

Our AI has been trained on thousands of real IELTS and TOEFL exams and calibrated with professional examiners. In blind tests, FluentIQ's scoring and feedback matched human examiners with 94% accuracy. Many students report that our AI provides more detailed and actionable feedback than human tutors.

How much time do I need to study with FluentIQ?

Most students see significant improvement with 30-60 minutes of daily practice. Our adaptive system maximizes your learning efficiency by focusing on your weak areas. Students typically see a 0.5-1.0 band score improvement in IELTS (or 10-15 points in TOEFL) within 4-6 weeks of consistent practice.

Can I access FluentIQ on mobile devices?

Yes! FluentIQ is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android that allow you to practice offline and sync your progress when you reconnect.

What if I'm not satisfied with FluentIQ?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you don't see improvement in your language skills or aren't satisfied with our platform for any reason, simply contact our support team for a full refund, no questions asked.

How does the AI speaking practice work?

Our AI speaking practice uses advanced speech recognition and natural language processing to analyze your pronunciation, fluency, grammar, and vocabulary. You'll speak into your device's microphone, and our AI will provide instant feedback on your performance, highlighting areas for improvement and suggesting specific exercises to enhance your speaking skills.
